## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the app and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your information:
   - **Username:** Unique username (used for display)
   - **Email:** Valid email address
   - **Password:** Minimum 6 characters
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be logged in automatically

### Logging In

1. Click **Sign In** on the home page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

## For Organizers

### Creating a Tournament

1. Go to **Dashboard** (top right)
2. Click **+ Create New**
3. Fill in tournament details:
   - **Tournament Name:** Give it a catchy name (e.g., "Summer Fighting Game Champ")
   - **Game:** Pick from 10+ supported games
   - **Max Players:** Choose 2, 4, 8, 16, or 32
   - **Registration Deadline:** When registration closes
4. Click **Create Tournament**

### Managing Your Tournament

**Dashboard** shows all your tournaments with:
- Player count (Current/Max)
- Tournament status
- Quick action buttons

### Locking the Bracket

1. Find your tournament on the dashboard
2. Click **View Details**
3. Once players have joined, click **Lock Bracket & Start**
4. The system automatically generates fair brackets

### Viewing the Bracket

1. Click **Bracket** on your tournament
2. See all matches organized by round
3. You'll see player names and match status

### Recording Match Results

1. Go to **Bracket** view
2. Find a pending match (no checkmark)
3. Click the player name who won (either P1 or P2)
4. System automatically advances winner to next round
5. When all matches in a round are done, next round appears

## For Players

### Browsing Tournaments

1. Go to **Home** page
2. See all active tournaments
3. View player count, game type, and organizer
4. Click **View Details** for more info

### Joining a Tournament

1. Find a tournament you want to join
2. Click **View Details**
3. Click **Join Tournament**
4. You're now registered! 🎉

### Checking Your Bracket

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Find your tournament
3. Click **Bracket** to see the bracket
4. Find your match and see opponent name
5. Play your game and wait for organizer to update result

### Viewing Results

1. Check **Bracket** after your match
2. See winner highlighted in green
3. Watch progression as tournament continues
4. See final standings when tournament ends

## Features Explained

### Single Elimination

- Winner advances, loser is out
- Fair and quick format
- Good for 8-32 players
- Determines champion in 3-5 rounds

### Bye System

- If odd number of players, one gets a bye
- Bye = automatic advancement without playing
- Randomly assigned
- Fair distribution

### Auto-Progression

- Winners automatically move to next round
- All matches in a round must complete before next round
- No manual bracket manipulation needed

## FAQ

**Q: Can I cancel after joining?**  
A: Before bracket locks, you can leave (contact organizer). After locking, you're committed.

**Q: What if someone doesn't show up for their match?**  
A: Contact the organizer. They can manually update the result.

**Q: Can I join multiple tournaments?**  
A: Yes! You can join as many tournaments as you want.

**Q: How are players seeded?**  
A: Randomly for fairness. We don't use rankings.

**Q: Can organizers change tournament details?**  
A: Only before bracket is locked. Once locked, it's fixed.

**Q: What's the maximum players?**  
A: 32 players (5 rounds).

**Q: How long do tournaments take?**  
A: Depends on timing. Usually 2-4 hours for 8 players.

**Q: Is there a chat feature?**  
A: Not yet, but we're adding it soon!

**Q: Can I delete a tournament?**  
A: Contact support. You can't self-delete to prevent misuse.

**Q: Are there mobile apps?**  
A: Not yet. The web app is fully responsive on phones.
